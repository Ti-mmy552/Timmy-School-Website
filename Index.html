About LMSC

Livingstone Academy was established on September 13, 1999, by its founder, Dr. Kolawole Olusola Christwealth, who was motivated by a divine vision to provide affordable, quality education. The college aims to foster students' creativity and wisdom to raise a new generation that will uphold standards and contribute to societal development.

Our Mission

To nurture creativity and foster the mindset needed for innovation and restructuring society.

Our Vision

To raise a new generation of leaders, game-changers, and innovators who can bring about positive change and development.

Our Core Values

Focus on Wisdom and Knowledge:

The college promotes an environment where students embrace knowledge and wisdom to uphold the standards of a great society.

Empowerment:

The college focuses on nurturing key individuals who will play significant roles in bringing about societal change and innovation.

Purpose-Driven Education:

Livingstone College is dedicated to raising a purpose-driven generation motivated towards innovation and new ideas.

Junior Secondary Curriculum

  • Core Subjects: Mathematics, English, Basic Science, Social Studies
  • Vocational: ICT, Agriculture, Home Economics
  • Languages: French, Yoruba (optional)
  • Assessment: Continuous Assessment (40%) + Exams (60%)

Special Programs

  • ICT integration in all subjects
  • Reading enhancement program
  • Leadership training for JSS3 students
